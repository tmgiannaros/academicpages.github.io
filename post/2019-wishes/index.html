20+19 quotes for the new year

Oct 28, 2018 3 min read
  1. “Opportunities don’t happen. You create them.” (Chris Grosser)
  2. “Success is the sum of small efforts, repeated day-in and day-out.” (Rober Collier)
  3. “The only place where success comes before work is in the dictionary.” (Vidal Sassoon)
  4. “When everything seems to be going against you, remember that the airplane takes off against the wind, not with it.” (Henry Ford)

  12. “You don’t have to be great to start, but you have to start to be great.” (Zig Ziglar)
  13. “It does not matter how slowly you go as long as you do not stop.” (Confucius)
  14. “Hardships often prepare ordinary peopel for extraordinary things” (C.S. Lewis)
  15. “Start by doing what’s necessary; then do what’s possible; and suddenly you are doing the impossible.” (Francis of Assisi)
  16. “I am not a product of my circumstances. I am a product of my decisions.” (Stephen Covey)
  17. “Don’t wish it were easier; Wish you were better.” (Jim Rhon)
  18. “Only those who dare to fail greatly can achieve greatness.” (Robert F. Kennedy)
  19. “I never dreamed about success. I worked for it.” (Estee Lauder)

  21. “Challenges are what make life interesting and overcoming them is what makes life meaningful.” (Joshua J. Marine)

  32. “A successful man is one who can lay a firm foundation with the bricks others have thrown at him.” (David Brinkley)
  33. “The fruit of your own hard work is the sweetest.” (Deepika Padukone)
  34. “A dream does not become reality through magic; it takes sweat, determination and hard work.” (Colin Powell)
  35. “The difference between greed and ambition is a greedy person desires things he isn’t prepared to work for.” (Habeeb Akande)
  36. “There are no shortcuts to any place worth going.” (Beverly Sills)
  37. “The difference between ordinary and extraordinary is that little extra.” (Jimmy Johnson)
  38. “Your friends will believe in your potential, your enemies will make you live up to it.” (Tim Fargo)
  39. “Ideas do not work..It is YOU who has to do the work.” (Manoj Arora)
